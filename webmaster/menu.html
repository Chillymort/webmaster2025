Bunuelos Colombianos

Also known as Colombian cheese fritters,
they are sweet-savory donuts among Latin America,

Served with house made dulce de leche (jam).

they are made from yuca roots of the cassava vegetable
They are prepared just as french fries are, with the
option for them to be boiled, mashed, or fried.

Chorípan

Also known as a chorizo sandwich, or french bread, it contains chorizo sausage on a bread roll called the marraqueta, and seasoned with prebre. Upon request, can be prepared with chimchurri sauce.

Ceviche de Verduras

Also known as vegetarian ceviche, it is a salad composed of mozzarella cheese, marinated onions, hot chile peppers, Andean choclo corn, cilantro, and lettuce. Cauliflower, and chickpeas may be added upon request.

Pabellon Criollo

A traditional beans and rice dish of Venezuela which contains black beans, rice, and slices of flank steak paired with freshly cooked tomatoes. Comes with slices of fried plantain (tajadas), or a fried egg on top (cabello).

$ 4.50

Pasta con Salsa Caruso

Also known as Uruguayan pasta with Caruso sauce, it is a Uruguayan dish with thick, creamy, native sauce, mushrooms, ham, beef broth, and cheese sauce.

$ 4.50

Pastel de Choclo

Also known as Chilean beef and corn casserole, it is a dish containing beef, onions, aisins, olives, and roasted chicken; it is topped with creamed choclo corn.

$ 4.50

Pollo Saltado

It is a chicken dish with tender beef, potatoes, red bell pepper, tomatoes, and onions. Fish, shrimp, or mushrooms can be added upon request.

Drinks

Iced/Hot Water

State whether you would like it hot, or cold upon ordering.

Papelón con Limón

Also known as panela with lemon. Made from water, limes, and piloncillo, which is a minimally processed, unrefined cane sugar, similar to brown sugar.

Mate de Coca

Coca tea, made from the coca plant. It has a slight bitter taste to that of matcha, but with a hint of sweetness.

Daterra Coffee

A type of sustainable Brazilian coffee, known for it's "greeness".

La Tizana

A tropical Venezuelan fruit drink (juice). Made traditionally with grenadine (granadina), requested fruits, orange juice, and limemade.

Aguas Frescas

Also known as panela with lemon. Made from water, limes, and piloncillo, which is a minimally processed, unrefined cane sugar, similar to brown sugar.

Guarapo (Non-Alcoholic)

This non-alcoholic version of the drink is made of pressed sugarcane juice, water, and fresh limes.

Leche con Plátano

In English, this drink is known as "milk and bananas", and is essentially a banana milkshake. It is made with fresh bananas, milk, sugar, and topped with whipped cream.

Desserts

Chocotorta

"Also known as "chocolate cake", this cake has a queso crema and dulce de leche filling, sandiwched between coffee-softened chocolate wafer cookies.

Espumillas

Served inside an icecream cone, this Ecuadorian dessert is sweet meringue cream topped with sweet-tart blackberry syrup. Can be served with paila ice cream (Helado de paila).

Picarones

A Peruvian dessert similar to an American donut, but with squash and sweet potato, covered with chancaca syrup.

Quesillo

Also called flan, or crème caramel, this Venezuelan dessert is made from egg and condensed milk, topped with caramel syrup.
